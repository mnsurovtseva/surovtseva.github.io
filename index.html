Borscht

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: hot
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 1 kg
Beet 2
Carrots 3

Step 1:Peel all vegetables

Step 2:Peel all vegetables

Step 3:Heat a large soup pot

Step 4:Add 8 cups broth and 2 cups water. Add sliced potatoes and sliced carrots then cook for 10-15 minutes or until easily pierced with a fork.

Step 5:While potatoes are cooking, place a large skillet over medium/high heat and add 2 Tbsp oil. Add chopped onion, celery and bell pepper. Saute stirring occasionally until softened and lightly golden (7-8 minutes). Add 4 Tbsp Ketchup and stir fry 30 seconds then transfer to the soup pot to continue cooking with the potatoes.

Step 6:When potatoes and carrots reach desired softness, add 1 can of beans with their juice, 2 bay leaves, 2-3 Tbsp white vinegar, 1 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper, 1 pressed garlic clove, and 3 Tbsp chopped dill. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes and add more salt and vinegar to taste.

Blueberry Syrup

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: cold
Ingredients Quantity
Blueberries 2 cups
Apple juice 1 cup
Sugar 1/3 cup
Cornstarch 4 tsp
Lemon juice 1 Tbsp

Step 1: Add blueberries, 1 cup juice and sugar to a small saucepan, stir. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Reduce heat to low and let simmer 6 â€“ 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until many of the blueberries have burst.

Step 3: In a small bowl whisk together remaining 2 Tbsp juice with the cornstarch then pour into pan. Let simmer 1 minute longer to thicken while stirring constantly.

Step 4: Remove from heat. Stir in lemon juice.
